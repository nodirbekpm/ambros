ABOUT CRPS-1

What is Complex Regional Pain Syndrome – 1

Complex regional pain syndrome (CRPS) is a condition that causes pain, changes in skin color and other symptoms in a certain part of your body — usually in your extremities. Your extremities include your arm, leg, hand or foot.

ABOUT CRPS-1

What is Complex Regional Pain Syndrome – 1

Complex regional pain syndrome (CRPS) is a condition that causes pain, changes in skin color and other symptoms in a certain part of your body — usually in your extremities. Your extremities include your arm, leg, hand or foot.

Cumulative estimated patient exposure of 500,000+ to date

Our Mission

Ambros is dedicated to advancing clinical-stage therapies into pharma pipelines

Our mission is to bring to market First-in-class product with no direct competition. Opportunity to establish standard of care with novel, disease-modifying mechanism of action.

Comparable pricing to small molecule therapies for rare diseases, which are often minimally efficacious.

To provide hope for patients with CRPS through targeted innovation.
